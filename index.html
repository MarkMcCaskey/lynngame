Pick a game.

Bubble Tap

Tap groups of same-colored bubbles to pop them. Bigger groups score more. Clear the board for a bonus.

Drop & Merge

Drop numbers into columns. Same numbers stacked together merge upward. Don't overflow.

Color Flood

Tap a color to grow your top-left region. Paint the whole board in as few moves as you can.

Three relaxing games. Each remembers your scores and supports undo.
